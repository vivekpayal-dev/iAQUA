About iAqua

Engineering the
Underwater Future

We are a multi-national team of design engineers from England, Finland, India, France, Thailand, and China. First and foremost, our talented team are passionate creators and enthusiasts dedicated to bringing the latest developments in technology to the underwater world.

00

Years of
Experience

Our team has 25 years of automotive design and manufacturing experience, having worked in partnership with many of the world's leading automotive brands including General Motors, Tesla, Audi, Volkswagen, Nissan, Lamborghini, Bentley, and more.

We have used that engineering and manufacturing experience to create a range of high-performance, technologically cutting-edge products crafted with the same precision demanded by our automotive customers. Combined with affordable prices and exceptional customer service, we deliver an uncompromising customer experience.

We are obsessed with providing high-performance watercraft manufactured to the same rigorous standards of quality and reliability seen in premium cars. With our zero-fail policy, we design and manufacture all critical components in-house. Even after several years, if a part needs replacing, the AquaDart range is designed for easy component replacement without needing to return the watercraft to the manufacturer.

No other dive scooter manufacturer matches AquaDart's level of performance, reliability, and modular design — giving you utmost confidence and many years of enjoyment.
